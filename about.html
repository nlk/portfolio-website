MY STORY

I have a flair for project planning. In my work, I have gotten good feedback on my abilities to get projects and the participants organized, starting a dialogue between the participating professional groups, and get everyone focused on a common goal; helping the project group focus, get a common vision, and start better cooperation. I have a keen eye for aesthetics and details, and have the ability to solve problems with accuracy and attention to detail in a creative and effective way through my experience working in architecture. I work concentrated at the task I'm given and work well in groups, but also independently. I enjoy social life and activities with my coworkers which I believe provides a good work atmosphere. I have 3 years of professional experience in architecture, and I believe there are many similarities between this field of work and UX design:
• Understanding the user and target groups, and creating great designs for them.
• Creating results that are easy to use functionally and intuitively.
• Organizing and structuring elements with the intention of giving the users flow and a simple layout to understand.
Hence has my previous experience with design projects given me a headstart in understanding and working with UX and how to approach a new project with a clear (but flexible) plan.
